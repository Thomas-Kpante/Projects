
# SUBWAY SIMPLE ORDER APP iOS

## Overview

This project is a mobile application developed using Xcode with Storyboard. It allows users to select their preferred type of bread and meat for a sandwich order, confirm their choices, and view a summary of their order.

## Features

- **Home Page**: The home page displays a "Start" button to begin the order process.
- **Bread Selection Page**: Users can choose from a variety of bread options presented as buttons.
- **Meat Selection Page**: After selecting bread, users proceed to choose from different meat options.
- **Confirmation Page**: Users confirm their bread and meat selections before finalizing the order.
- **Order Summary Page**: Upon confirmation, users can view a summary of their order.
- **Thank You Page**: After viewing the summary, users are presented with a "Thank You" page.

## Installation

1. Clone or download the repository.
2. Open the project in Xcode.
3. Run the project on a simulator or physical device.

## Usage

1. On the home page, tap the "Start" button to begin the order process.
2. On the bread selection page, choose your preferred type of bread by tapping the corresponding button.
3. Proceed to the meat selection page where you can select your desired meat.
4. After selecting bread and meat, confirm your choices on the confirmation page.
5. View the order summary to review your selections.
6. Tap the "Thank You" button to complete the order process.
